Cachapa

Description

Cachapa is a delectable Venezuelan dish made from fresh corn batter. It is similar to a pancake, but with a unique twist. The batter is cooked on a griddle until golden and slightly crispy. Cachapas are typically served folded, filled with cheese, and enjoyed as a sweet or savory delight.

Ingredients

  • 450 grams of fresh or frozen corn
  • 130 ml of milk
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of corn flour
  • 2 tablespoons of weath flour
  • 1 tablespoon of withe sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of butter, and a littel extra for the pan and to spread on the cachapa
  • Withe cheese
  • Salt

Preparation

  • Mix the corn (unfrozan if needed), milk, sugar and sat in a blender.
  • Now mix the eggs and the butter inside the blender with the corn mix
  • In case the mix is too liquid, add some of the weath flour to the blender
  • Put the mix on a closed container on the fridge
  • Spread some butter on a hot pan to melt it
  • Add the cachapa mix with a ladle on the pan. Use the ladle to make it plain and about 0.5cm thick
  • Flip the cachapa after 2-3 minutes.
  • Wait 2 more minutes and take it out from the pan<./li>
  • Spread butter, add a lot of white cheese and enjoy!
