Ingredients

For the black beans:

  • 500gr of black beans.
  • 4 slices of bacon.
  • 1 medium onion.
  • 1/2 red bell pepper.
  • 3 garlic cloves.
  • 125gr of papelon.
  • Black pepper.
  • Olive oil.

For the rice:

  • 175gr of white round rice
  • 1/2 red bell pepper.
  • 1/2 onion.
  • Salt.
  • Olive oil.
  • 2 garlic cloves.

For the meat:

  • 1/2 kg of beef skirt steak
  • 1/2 red bell pepper.
  • 4 tomatoes.
  • 1 big onion.
  • 4 garlic cloves.
  • 2 sweet peppers.
  • 1 tablespoon of smashed tomatoes.
  • Black pepper.
  • Ground cumin.
  • English sauce.
  • Salt.
  • Olive oil.

For the bananas:

  • 2 big male bananas to fry.
  • Olive oil.

Preparation

Preparation of the black beans

  1. The night before the meal wash the beans and let them in bowl with water.
  2. The next day take te beans out of the water (taking out the bad ones).
  3. Put the beans in a pot full of water.
  4. Chop the onion and the bell pepper, put them with the beans with medium fire.
  5. Cook for at least an hour.

Preparation of the meat

  1. Chop the onion and the garlic cloves.
  2. Put the meat inside a pot with water, the onion and de garlic.
  3. Cook for around 3 hours.
  4. When it's cold, use your hand to make the meat appear like thin hair.
  5. Pour some olive oil on a frying pan, add chopped onion, garlic, both peppers and let them cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the chopped and smashed tomatoes.
  7. Finally add the meat, move and let them cook.

Continuing with the beans

  1. When the beans are a little bit soft, add the shredded papelon and spme cumin.
  2. Keep coocking the beans and add more water if necessary.
  3. Add in another frying pan spread with olive oil the bacon, 3 chopeed garlic cloves and a shredded half onion. Coock 5 minutes.
  4. Add this to the beans, letting it all coock for 15 more minutes.

Preparation of the rice

  1. Chop an onion and 2 garlic cloves.
  2. Put a pot in the fire with olive oil, the chopped bell pepper, onion, garlic and coock for 2 minutes. Then add the rice.
  3. Move it and add 2 parts of water for every part of rice.
  4. When it's boiling put a lower flame and put a cover on the pot. When it's coocked put it ourt of the fire.

Final dish

  1. To finish, we only need too fry the banana. Cut it in long slices about 1cm thick.
  2. Put a frying pan with olive oil, fry the bananas with a high flame and retire to a dish with absorbing paper.
  3. Put in a dish a portion of the meat, the rice, the beans and a couple slices of the fried banana. Enjoy!
